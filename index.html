Welcome to SermonReach

Turn any Sunday sermon into a full week of social media content. Fill in the sermon details, paste the YouTube link, choose your clip focus, and click Analyze Sermon. Results appear in three tabs below.

Start Here

Fill in the speaker title, first name, last name, and the sermon date. The sermon date is required. Paste the YouTube link. SermonReach will fetch the transcript automatically. If it fails, click Paste transcript manually, go to YouTube, click the three dots under the video, click Show Transcript, copy it, and paste it here.

Sermon Clip Focus

Choose one before analyzing. Outreach finds five clips for people outside the faith. Discipleship finds five clips for growing believers. Custom lets you enter a specific question your community is asking.

Sermon Summary Tab

A 500 word narrative summary ready to post as a text caption on Instagram, Facebook, or YouTube. Use Copy Summary or Download to save it.

Sermon Notes Slides Tab

Choose a theme from the four thumbnails. Click Generate Slide Images to export the full deck as an HTML file you can screenshot. Use Download PNG on any individual slide for a single image. All slides are 1080x1080 for Instagram carousel.

Sermon Clips Tab

Five video clips with timestamps and captions. Edit the caption before creating the clip. Click Create Clip to send that moment to Opus Clip. Send no more than 3 clips at a time. When the clip is ready, click Publish Now to post immediately or Schedule to pick a future date and time. Green badge means published. Gold badge means scheduled.

Analysis History

Click the hamburger menu at the top right and select Analysis History to see your last 10 analyses. Click Load on any entry to restore the full results instantly.

My Account and Sign Out

Click the hamburger menu at the top right. Choose My Account to update your password or Sign Out to log out.

Welcome to SermonReach

Turn any Sunday sermon into social media content. Fill in the sermon details, pick your clip focus, and tap Analyze Sermon.

Getting Around

Use the bar at the bottom of the screen to navigate. Start Here is your input form. Results shows your analysis. History shows past analyses. Account gives you settings and sign out.

Before You Analyze

Fill in the speaker name and sermon date at the top of Start Here. Paste the YouTube link. Swipe through the three focus cards and tap one: Outreach, Discipleship, or Custom. Tap Analyze Sermon and wait about 30 to 45 seconds.

Viewing Your Results

After the analysis finishes, the app switches to Results automatically. Swipe left and right to move between Sermon Summary, Sermon Notes Slides, and Sermon Clips.

Sermon Clips

Each clip has a caption you can edit before creating it. Tap Create Clip to send it to Opus Clip. When it is ready, tap Publish Now to post immediately or Schedule to pick a date and time. Green badge means published. Gold badge means scheduled.

Analysis History

Tap History in the bottom bar to see your last 10 analyses. Tap Load to bring any of them back up instantly.

Account and Sign Out

Tap Account in the bottom bar. From there you can open My Account, get Help, or Sign Out.
